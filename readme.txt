== Description ==

Quip Invoices is a complete invoicing solution built directly into your WordPress website. Create and send invoices, accept payments, store client information and track your sales with Quip Invoices.
Built from the ground up to cover all the invoicing needs of a small business, Quip Invoices is easy and intuitive to use, quick to get started and packed full of features.

The free edition of Quip Invoices has the following features:

1. Create fully-featured invoices
1. Invoice options include line-item level adjustments, tax rates, customizable company info & logo and more.
1. Accept credit card payments directly. Powered by Stripe.
1. Store client details for easy re-use.
1. Automated tracking of when clients view and pay your invoices.
1. Choose your own invoice numbers or just use the auto-generated ones.
1. Special print styles so invoices look good on paper as well as on screen.

[Upgrading to the paid version of Quip Invoices](http://codecanyon.net/item/quip-invoices-fully-featured-wordpress-invoicing/11996217?tag=QuipCode) will give you these extra features:

1. Create quotes with optional deadlines.
1. Send invoices to multiple people with the click of a button.
1. Customizable email templates with dynamic tags for invoice data.
1. Accept partial payments.
1. Copy invoices with a click.
1. Convert quotes to invoices with a click.
1. Export all your data to CSV files.
1. Manually add payments to invoices (e.g. for mail/phone payments)
1. Add notes and payment instructions to invoices.
1. Full customer support.
1. Regular software updates.
1. Translated to 3 languages! Spanish, French & German
1. Email notifications for client actions like viewing and paid invoices.


[Check out the paid version of Quip Invoices](http://codecanyon.net/item/quip-invoices-fully-featured-wordpress-invoicing/11996217?tag=QuipCode)

We have a demo at [invoicingplugin.com](http://invoicingplugin.com) where you can see all the premium features in action.

== Installation ==

1. Upload plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Follow the on screen instructions in the Quick Start documentation
1. You're now ready to create invoices!
